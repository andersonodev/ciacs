Build a complete, visually stunning institutional website for 
CIACS — Centro de Integração da Arte e Cultura dos Surdos, 
a Brazilian nonprofit Deaf arts and culture institution 
founded in 1989 in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. 
All text content must be in Brazilian Portuguese.

IDENTITY AND CONTEXT
This institution has 35+ years of history promoting Deaf 
art, culture, and identity in Brazil. The site must 
communicate impact, history, and institutional credibility. 
It serves the Deaf community, academics, government bodies 
like DIPEBS/MEC, and potential partners. The tone is 
cultural, warm, and professional — not corporate.

DESIGN SYSTEM
Primary: #2E7D32 (deep green from logo)
Secondary: #1565C0 (blue from logo)
Accent: #F9A825 (warm gold)
Background: #FFFFFF and #F5F5F5
Text: #1A1A1A
Use bold typography for impact. Be creative with layouts — 
asymmetric sections, full-width imagery, overlapping 
elements, and visual hierarchy that feels editorial and 
cultural rather than generic. Subtle animations and hover 
effects are welcome.
