COMPLETE VISUAL REDESIGN. Discard everything from the 
previous version. Build a world-class institutional 
website for CIACS — Centro de Integração da Arte e 
Cultura dos Surdos, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, founded 1989.

DESIGN REFERENCE AND AMBITION
Design at the level of: Museu de Arte do Rio (MAR), 
MASP São Paulo, or MoMA.org. Think editorial magazine 
meets cultural institution. Bold, sophisticated, 
contemporary. This is not a school project or NGO 
template — it is a cultural landmark's digital home.

ABSOLUTE RULES — NO EXCEPTIONS
- Zero emojis anywhere in the entire site
- Zero stock illustration icons
- No playful or rounded UI elements
- No rainbow color cards with different colors per card
- No childish or casual typography
- No generic bootstrap or material design look

TYPOGRAPHY
Headlines: Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond — 
large, bold, editorial serif with cultural weight
Body: Inter or DM Sans — clean, readable, modern
Hierarchy: extreme contrast between headline sizes 
and body sizes. Headlines should be dramatic.
Never use the same font weight for everything.

COLOR SYSTEM — STRICT
Primary surface: #FFFFFF (white)
Secondary surface: #F7F4EF (warm off-white, like 
aged paper or linen)
Primary brand: #1B5E20 (deep forest green — dignified, 
not bright)
Secondary brand: #0D3B6E (deep navy blue)
Gold accent: #C8960C (muted gold, not yellow)
Text: #1A1A1A (near black)
Muted text: #5C5C5C
Never use bright colors. Everything should feel rich 
and mature. Section backgrounds alternate between 
white and warm off-white only. No colored section 
backgrounds except the hero and one CTA banner.

HEADER
Minimal, elegant. White background with thin bottom 
border. CIACS logo left. Navigation right in small 
caps, Inter, letter-spacing 0.1em. On scroll: header 
gets subtle shadow. Active nav item: gold underline.
No colored header background.
